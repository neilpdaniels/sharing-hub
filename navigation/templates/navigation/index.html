Why SharingHub exists

Of the few ways to own precious metals (Physical, ETFs, Registered ownership), Physical ownership has many advantages. SharingHub exists to increase the efficiency of dealing in physically delivered coins and bars, with an aim of cutting out the middle men and so reducing the spread between the buying and selling prices. Our aim is to be the cheapest place to safely buy, and the most rewarding place to safely sell.

We also look to capture and display other prices that are avaliable through other vendors, to provide a single, transparent view of available prices. We have no affiliation to these, and earn no commission.

Any transactions agreed over SharingHub will transact over Transpact, an FCA registered escrow service, who in turn will appoint an independant arbitrator, should it be needed

Q&A

Who is Buying / Selling?

Within the internal marketplace you'll be buing from, or selling to, other users of SharingHub. SharingHub does also make markets, we'll do so using our own inventory with a primary intention of increasing liquidity.

Is SharingHub safe?

SharingHub is a matching service and is not involved in the settlement of transactions/ payment of monies. We have integrated our transactions with an FCA approved Escrow service, and no money is released by them until the item has been verified by the buyer. Please see our guides for more information about the transaction workflow and recommended verification steps.

Is SharingHub the best price?

In addition to our internal market, we also quote the prices from leading external websites, where they make their prices available for scraping. We have no affiliation to them, the intention is to make prices fully transparent.

Is VAT inclusive in the prices?

VAT is either inclusive, or not attracted. Gold has no VAT applicable, whereas order in other metals will be priced with any applicable VAT. Private sellers are unlikely to be VAT registered unless they deal in larger quanties.

Are all products Certified and provided in tamper-proof casing?

No, although we would suggest that those new to investing in physical metals buy pre-certified items (in tamper proof casing) else complete full verification with a local expert. We only list products certified by ANACS, ICG, NGC or PCGS.
